A mathematical educational game designed for grades 4-6

Project Background

Finding a number's factors, the numbers that divide it exactly with no remainder, is an important math skill that is foundational to division and used throughout math from Middle School through University. FACTORBS is an arcade game that aims to teach students the skills required to identify factors in an engaging and effective manner. In the game, snakes of orbs will approach on a set path, and the player must stop them from reaching the end by clearing the orbs. They do this by firing orbs from a cannon, which can be controlled using the mouse. When a fired orb hits a snake orb that it is a factor of, the snake orbâ€™s value is divided by the number of the fired orb. The player must be careful as hitting the wrong orb causes the fired orb to be inserted into the snake, making it longer. The more orbs the player can clear, the higher their score at the end of the game.
