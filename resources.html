Resources

Domain Research

To ensure the game fits well within the mathematics curriculum of the target audience, being kids in grades 4 to 6, we consulted two resources: The Massachusetts Mathematics Curriculum Framework, and the Common Core State Standards For Mathematics. Both of these resources are linked below.

Massachusetts Mathematics Curriculum Framework: https://www.doe.mass.edu/frameworks/math/2017-06.pdf

Common Core State Standards For Mathematics: https://corestandards.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/Math_Standards1.pdf
